WHAT IS A LUX MUNDIS CUP 2024

Tangub City Global College(TCGC), proudly present the Lux Mundis Cup 2024, Theme:"Majestic Fusion". This exciting five-days event will showcase a dynamic blend of academic,sport and special activities designde to foster education growth promote knowledge sharing, and enhance critical thinking skills. Above all, the event aims to nurture camaraderie and strengthen the bond among student across the academic community.

Day 1 of the Lux Mundis Cup 2024 was filled with unforgettable moment, from the Thankgiving Eucharistic Mass that set a spiritual tone to the event to the majestic Parade.

The opening salvo brought energy to the crowd, followed by a dazzling fireworks display that lit up the night sky.
